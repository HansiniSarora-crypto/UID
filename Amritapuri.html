About the Amritapuri Campus

Situated in the serene village of Vallikavu, our campus exudes a sense of warmth and tranquility, creating the perfect environment
for intellectual growth and personal development. Our educational institution encompasses six prestigious schools and more than 20
research centres, catering to students passionate about various disciplines such as arts, business, humanities, natural sciences,
and social sciences. With a sprawling 80-acre expanse of land, our campus provides ample space for learning, exploration, and recreation.
The million square feet of built-up area includes state-of-the-art academic blocks, comfortable student hostels, and a range of modern
amenities to enhance the overall campus experience.

All Schools and Research Centers on campus are committed to generating, disseminating and preserving knowledge. They seek to develop
in each member of the campus, the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively and effectively for the betterment of humanity.
Numerous seminars, discussions and debates help enhance the quest for knowledge.

Distinguished professors and scholars share their knowledge and experiences with the students.
The campus provides all modern amenities from playgrounds to cafeterias.
