Vision and Mission
Five Pillars of Academy

National Defence Academy has emerged as an iconic Military Academy over years,
attracting not only the finest young men from all corners of the country but also
from friendly foreign countries. In the seven decades of its glorious existence,
National Defence Academy has grown both in grace and grandeur. From its portals
have graduated the 'The Leaders of the men', who have proved their worth and have
demonstrated the essence of inter-services camaraderie and jointmanship, thereby
vindicating the faith and vision of its founding fathers.
