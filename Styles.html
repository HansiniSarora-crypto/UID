"Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham"

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a premier private, multi-disciplinary, 
               
               research-intensive university in India with an 'A++' rating from NAAC and a top-10 ranking in NIRF 2024.

                 Founded in 1994 by Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma), it operates 10 campuses across India,
                
                 (including Coimbatore, Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Kochi, and Amaravati). 
                
                 The university serves over 30,000 students, offering 250+ programs in engineering.
