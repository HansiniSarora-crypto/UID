About the Faridabad campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (also known as Amrita University), Faridabad established in 2022 is a
Deemed to be University. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Faridabad is approved by NMC (MCI), and is
accredited by NAAC-A++. Admission in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Faridabad is offered in Engineering,
Management and Science at UG/ PG/ Doctorate level.
